## Services & Tools

### [Listbrew](https://github.com/pritkr/listbrew/tree/develop/listbrew)
**Realtime Auto sync from Frappe to Listmonk.**   
No more manual CSV exports and newsletter list always stays up-to-date to any frappe app data.
